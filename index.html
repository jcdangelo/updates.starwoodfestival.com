Announcements

  • Starwood 2026 dates posted - July 14-20. Tickets ($50 online advance discount ends June 30, 2026, midnight ET)
  • Food options onsite:
    • NOTE: Caffiena's kitchen food line will not be serving food to attendees for Starwood 2026.
    • Caffiena's cafe will have a number of food options including some from Suzy's BBQ: pulled pork sandwiches, brats, hotdogs, hamburgers from Century Farm Market, breakfast sandwiches, baked goods, sides and more, some hot and cold drinks, ice.
    • There will be food trucks next to Caffiena's including:
      • Burrito Buggy: burritos and other street mexican food (a href="https://www.facebook.com/burritobuggy/">FB page).
      • The Completely Baked Pretzel Company: pretzel gyros, wraps, fajitas, tots, smoothies and more (FB page).
  • Starwood Attendees may arrive before Tuesday, July 14 to camp at a $25/adult/day rate as early as Friday, July 10. NOTE: Prior to the event, Wisteria will operate the gate and no entrance will be permitted between 10pm and 10am; there will be a new electronically locked gate new to Starwood in 2026. Please camp outside of taped off reserved areas.
  • Torch Makers Needed! - Starwood will be looking for 5 volunteers to create the torches that will be used to start the Bonfire! Ask at Information before 6pm Tue of Starwood (July 14). Learn more.
  • The Starwood XLVI (2026) Tour Guide (5.7 MB) was sent to the printers July 1. Some errata found so far.

  • The Starwood Choir wants your help to JOIN THEM and help with chants at songs at Opening and Closing rituals and for the Saturday night concert. Show up at Opening and Closing circles early to learn the chants and to daily 11:30am-12:45pm rehearsals at Didge Dome to join them on the Main Stage on Saturday!
