Become a Torch Maker!

All torch makers for 2026 have been selected. Inquire about becoming a torch maker for Starwood XLVII in 2027. Visit the Starwood information booth in front of Caffeinas and ask to join the torch makers by 6pm Tuesday, July 14, 2026! More will be solicited at the Opening Ceremony if needed, but don't miss your chance!

Time commitment: About 2 hours a day starting around 9am for 3 days, on Wed, Thu and Fri to create the torch and concluding with the torch naming and passing ceremony Friday afternoon at 7pm in the Stone Circle.

The process involves you joining a group of 8 total individuals and instructor who will lead you on the time honored tradition of crafting a torch from local wood, weaving in your own energy and creativity that will be showcased on stage and in the bonfire ceremony. Some mild hand crafting involved.

One remaining volunteer is needed, and these spots tend to go fast, so don't delay.
