Starwood Choir Improv Specialist: Dr. Kirk Andrew Everist teaches improvisation and theatre at Austin College. As the coach of The Austin College Improv Troupe he guides artists in improvising songs and dance as well as scenes and characters. He's trained in choral singing, musical theatre, and transformative chant for over 50 years.

Starwood Choir Director: Nellie James has lived a life singing, from folk music to opera, from sacred to secular and everything in between. She is well versed on the music of Saturday night and the music of Sunday morning, all presented in the joy of making music with people. From 2008 to 2020, she co-directed SATB (a different kind of Choir). A group of semiprofessional musicians who sang themed benefit concerts for local charities. For the last 5 years she has been the Music Director for the Unitarian Universalist Fellowship of Athens.

Lyceum: A space dedicated to open learning and discussion. Come to teach, learn, share ideas, and engage in thoughtful conversation. Whether you have knowledge to offer or questions to explore, the Lyceum is an informal gathering place for education, dialogue, and intellectual exchange — no lectures, no hierarchy, just curious minds coming together. All are welcome to participate.
