Errors and omissions.

Errors and omissions in the Tour Guide since publication will be listed here. More important ones will also be displayed on whiteboards. See also bios.

Minor

  • The Starwood Parade on Saturday has been scheduled to start at 3:45pm this year. It is still listed as 4pm on page 10 under "Starwood Coop Child-Care and Rules For Minors."

  • "What is in the word: Gypsy?" is scheduled for Thursday, 5:15-6:30pm Oomphalos and listed that way under the bio for Koshek Swaminathan (Pg 43-44) and in the schedule (Pg 52), but incorrectly listed as for Friday on Pg 22, Pg 31, and Pg 34.

  • Pg 35 Under Dan Covan, and Pg 44 under Koshek Swaminathan, the "Dan and Yuka DJ Set" incorrectly lists Koshek Swaminathan as also presenting.

  • Clarification: on Pg 36, Judy Ann Nock & the Trance Senders: Thu 11pm - 3am Pufferdome is now a part of the Mad Pharaoh's Costume Ball (see Pg) scheduled 11pm-4am.

  • Clarification: the "Spirit Hunt (Ritual)" and "Sacred Hunt (Ritual)" are different names for the same ritual. Pg 39 "see the description of the Sacred Hunt Ritual" was not adjusted to say "Spirit Hunt Ritual".

  • Pg 51 "10:30pm PawPaw Firelighting - - Paw Paw- dawn" is to mean that the PawPaw Firelighting is at 10:30pm on Tuesday, and the all night drumming lasts until dawn every night. There are nightly lightings Wed-Fri around 9:30pm ~ 10:30pm. On Saturday, PawPaw resumes around 4:20am after drumming at the Big Bonfire.

  • Pg 55 The "Bonfire Blessing" at Sat 6:15-6:45pm by "Starwood" has a description listed under Ian Corrigan on Pg 24-25.

Trivial

  • Pg 51 Yes, "Lyceum - Oomphalos" is in two consecutive slots on purpose! Consider them part of a continuous event. Wed 3:45-6:30pm Oomphalos. See Lyceum under bios.

  • Pg 52 "B. Llewellyn" is improperly short for Bobbi Llewellyn-Harper (and Peter D. Harper). See description under Pufferdome By Night (Pg 17).

  • Pg 54 Fri "9:00 - 11pm: Kirtan - K. Covan - Pufferdome" - should be D. Covan (or K. Swaminathan)
