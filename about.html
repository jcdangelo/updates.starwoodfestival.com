This simple low-bandwidth website was designed in mind of the Starwood attendee on site with very low connectivity via the on site wifi or mobile carrier network. As such, there are not many frills here. Just enough to get you timely updates should you find loading this page easier than walking into main camp to view the sign boards (that option is available for those who prefer to remain off-line). Introduced in 2025 for Starwood # 45.

The schedule on this website will be updated daily (or more often) as information becomes available. While every effort will be made to release a complete schedule in the Tour Guide printed booklet, there almost always is at least a few items that will need to be changed in light of the inevitable challenges that come with running such a large event with so many great contributors and in an outside location.

Let us know what you think! Email us feedback about this Updates website. Or include feedback about this site when you reply to the end-of festival overall feedback when that form becomes available.
